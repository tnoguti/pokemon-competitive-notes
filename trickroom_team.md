# Trick Room Game Plan

## 🟢 Default Lead (most games)
Clefable + Hatterene

Turn 1:
- Follow Me
- Trick Room

Goal: Guaranteed Trick Room setup

---

## 🟡 Anti-Fake Out Lead
Farigiraf + Hatterene

Turn 1:
- Trick Room (priority blocked automatically)

Goal: Beat Fake Out / Sucker Punch teams

---

## 🔴 Aggressive Lead
Incineroar + Hatterene

Turn 1:
- Fake Out (big threat)
- Trick Room

Goal: Stop damage + set TR

---

## ⚡ After Trick Room (TURN 2+)

Switch into:
- Mega Camerupt
- Kingambit

Then:
- Start attacking immediately
- You should move first

---

## 🔥 Win Condition

Turn cycle:
1. Set Trick Room
2. Bring in sweepers
3. KO as much as possible before TR ends

---

## ❗ If Trick Room fails

Fallback plan:
- Use Incineroar (Fake Out + Parting Shot)
- Reset positioning
- Try to set Trick Room again safely

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ Attacking Turn 1 instead of setting TR  
❌ Leaving Hatterene exposed  
❌ Not switching in Camerupt fast enough  
❌ Forgetting Trick Room turns (only 5 turns)

---

## ⏱ Trick Room Turn Tracking

Turn 1 = Setup  
Turn 2–4 = DAMAGE WINDOW  
Turn 5 = LAST TURN  

After that:
→ Reset or defend
